A Little Bit More About Me

I am a junior at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. I am majoring in Music and minoring in both Entrepreneurship and Korean. My unique set of studies connects to my unusual background. I come from a military household, and moving has always been a part of my life. Traveling has exposed me to diverse cultures, unique obstacles, and opportunities from a young age. Thanks to my experiences, my passions include learning, working with others, and innovating in all corners of my life.

Since 2015, I have been an active member in the online music space by creating content for myself and others in many mediums. Because of my involvement in this space, I learn new skills every day, and I greatly enjoy the fast-paced nature of the music and content creation communities.

With my unique interests and ideas, I aim to contribute to education reform in music technology, add new perspectives in the music industry, and be a front runner in the globalization of popular music.
